The Southeast Asia Club was established to connect Kellogg students from Southeast Asia or who are interested in Southeast Asia

Our members comprise Southeast Asians as well as students of nationalities from all over the world

Our Objectives

Recruting

We channel recruiting opportunities in Southeast Asia to the Kellogg community

Promote

We promote Kellogg among prospective students and recruiters in Southeast Asia

Network

We create a strong alumni network for the Southeast Asian population at Kellogg

Culture

We promote Southeast Asian culture to the greater Kellogg community
